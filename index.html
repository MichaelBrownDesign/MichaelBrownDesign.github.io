About

Hello!

I'm Michael Brown, a passionate game engineer currently employed at PlaySide Studios based in Melbourne, Australia. I graduated with an Advanced Diploma of Professional Game Development from the Academy of Interactive Entertainment in 2014 before continuing on to study game design. I joined a team of fellow graduates in 2019 at a studio called ManaTea where we made our first game Unfamiliar. After releasing Unfamiliar, I started a job at PlaySide Studios where I have been for the past two years and had the pleasure of working on Jumanji: Epic Run and World of Pets.

In my spare time I am usually working on game development tools (particularly for Unity) that can be used to streamline the development process or making prototypes that help me learn and improve my skills as a programmer, which regularly include areas like graphics programming, procedural generation and simulation.
