About

Hello!

I'm Michael Brown, a passionate game engineer currently employed at PlaySide Studios based in Melbourne, Australia. I graduated as a game programmer from the Academy of Interactive Entertainment in 2014 before continuing on to study game design. This led to founding an independent studio with my peers and releasing our first game Unfamiliar!

After release, I was lucky enough to land a job at PlaySide Studios where I have had the pleasure of working on Jumanji: Epic Run and World of Pets.
